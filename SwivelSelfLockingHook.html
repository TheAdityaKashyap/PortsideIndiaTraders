SHUR-LOC® Swivel Hook Overview

The Crosby® S-13326 SHUR-LOC® Swivel Hook with Anti-Friction Bearing is engineered for superior performance in demanding lifting applications. Manufactured from forged alloy steel and featuring a unique bearing design, this hook rotates freely under load while maintaining the safety of the SHUR-LOC® self-locking mechanism.

OSHA Approved for Personnel Lifting

Complies with OSHA 1926.1431(g)(1)(i)(A) and 1926.1501(g)(4)(iv)(B) when properly installed and locked

Key Features

  • Anti-friction bearing allows free rotation under load
  • Forged alloy steel construction - quenched & tempered
  • Self-locking latch engages automatically when loaded
  • Recessed trigger design prevents damage during operation
  • Large thumb access for easy operation with gloves
  • Approved for personnel lifting when properly installed
  • Fatigue rated: 20,000 cycles at 1.5× Working Load Limit
  • Individually proof tested at 2.5× WLL with certification
  • Rated for wire rope and Grade 80 / Grade 100 chains
  • Design Factor: 4:1 for chain (5:1 available for added safety)
  • Deformation indicators present for safety checks
  • Trigger repair kit available (S-4316)
