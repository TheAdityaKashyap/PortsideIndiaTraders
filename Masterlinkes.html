Master Links Overview

Our Crosby® master links are forged from high-strength alloy steel, quenched and tempered for superior durability. Designed with safety as the top priority, these master links feature a 5:1 design factor and are rigorously tested to exceed industry standards.

Maximum Safety

Individually proof tested with certification to ensure reliability in critical lifting operations

Grade 80/100 Performance

Designed for chain sling applications with Grade 80 and Grade 100 chain with permanent WLL markings.

Fatigue Rated
