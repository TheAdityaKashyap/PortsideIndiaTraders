Field Usage Recommendations

Regular Inspection

Determine lubrication needs through frequent examinations of wire rope condition. Look for signs of wear, corrosion, and dryness.

Re-lubrication Frequency

Frequency of application is more critical than quantity applied. Regular maintenance ensures optimal rope performance and longevity.

Application Note

Expect some field loss due to spillage and handling - use application guidelines as reference points rather than exact measures.

Core Penetration

Unlike greasy lubricants, Vitalife penetrates to the rope core for complete internal protection and lubrication.
