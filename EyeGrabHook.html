Grab Hook Overview

Crosby® Grab Hooks are engineered for superior performance in demanding lifting applications. Available in both Cradle Grab Hook and standard Grab Hook designs, these forged alloy steel hooks are quenched & tempered for maximum strength and durability.

Optimized for Chain Efficiency

Cradle design allows for 100% efficiency of Grade 100 chain

Key Features

  • Forged alloy steel construction – quenched & tempered
  • Proof tested to 2.5× Working Load Limit (WLL) with certification
  • Product Identification Code (PIC), size, and Crosby name engraved
  • Suitable for Grade 100 and Grade 80 chain
  • Fatigue rated to 20,000 cycles at 1.5× WLL
  • Cradle design allows full sling capacity in choker configuration
  • Design Factor: 4:1 for maximum safety
  • Durable construction for long service life
