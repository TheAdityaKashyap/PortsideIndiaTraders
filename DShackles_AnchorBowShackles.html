Shackle Overview

Crosby® Shackles are engineered for superior performance in demanding lifting applications. Available in various designs including bolt type chain shackles, screw pin anchor shackles, and more. These forged steel shackles are quenched & tempered for maximum strength and durability.

Key Features

  • Forged steel construction - quenched & tempered
  • Alloy pins for maximum strength
  • Working Load Limit and Grade 6 permanently shown on every shackle
  • Hot-dip galvanized (G) or self-colored (S) options
  • Fatigue rated to 20,000 cycles at 1.5x Working Load Limit
  • Approved for use in extreme temperatures (-40°C to 204°C)
  • Meets or exceeds ASME B30.26 requirements
  • Look for the Red Pin® - the mark of genuine Crosby quality

Technical Specifications

  • Design Factor: 6:1
  • Maximum Proof Load: 2 times the Working Load Limit
  • Temperature Range: -40°C (-40°F) to 204°C (400°F)
  • Fatigue Rating: 20,000 cycles at 1.5 times WLL
  • Certifications: ASME B30.26, EN13889:2003, ABS, DIN, Lloyds, and more
