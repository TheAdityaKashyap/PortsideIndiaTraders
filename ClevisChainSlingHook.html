Clevis Chain Hook Overview

The Crosby® S-314A Clevis Chain Hook with Integrated Latch is manufactured from high-strength forged alloy steel, quenched and tempered for superior performance. Designed for secure connections in demanding lifting applications, this hook features an integrated heavy-duty latch for enhanced safety.

Key Features

  • Forged alloy steel construction - quenched & tempered
  • Integrated heavy-duty latch for secure connections
  • Individually proof tested at 2.5× WLL with certification
  • Meets ASTM A952 standards for Grade 80 chain fittings
  • Fatigue rated to 20,000 cycles at 1.5× WLL
  • 4:1 Design Factor for Grade 80 Chain
  • Available in sizes for 6mm to 16mm chain
  • Replacement latches available for maintenance
