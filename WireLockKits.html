Socket Selection

  • Recommended for Crosby 416-417 Speller Sockets
  • Sockets must comply with Federal or International Standards
  • Rough finish inside socket may increase seating load

Seizing & Brooming

  • Seize wire rope using soft annealed iron wire
  • Unlay strands as far as seizing
  • Cut out any fiber core
  • Unlay individual wires down to seizing
3

Cleaning & Positioning

  • Clean according to lubricant/coating type
  • Comply with EPA regulations
  • Position socket over broom until wires are level with socket basket
  • Clamp rope and socket vertically
4
