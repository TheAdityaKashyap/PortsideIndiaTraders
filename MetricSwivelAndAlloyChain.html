Product Overview

Our Crosby® alloy chains and connecting links are forged from high-strength alloy steel, quenched and tempered for superior durability. Designed with safety as the top priority, these products feature a 4:1 design factor and are rigorously tested to exceed industry standards.

Maximum Strength

25% stronger than Grade 80 alloy chain for enhanced safety

Industry Compliant

Meets NACM and ASTM A952/A973M standards for Grade 10 chain

Premium Construction

Forged alloy steel construction, quenched and tempered

Secure Connection

LOK-A-LOY® connecting links with locking system for simple assembly

Grade 10 Performance

25% stronger than Grade 80 alloy chain with permanent identification markings.

Protective Finish

Black paint finish for enhanced corrosion resistance in demanding environments.

Fatigue Rated

Rated for extended service life with proof testing at 2 times Working Load Limit.

Safety Factor

4:1 Design Factor with maximum proof load at 2 times the Working Load Limit.

Key Product Features

  • Peerless 10 Alloy Chain: 25% stronger than Grade 80 alloy chain
  • Permanently embossed with "P" (Peerless) and "10" (Grade) for identification
  • Black paint finish for corrosion resistance
  • Meets latest NACM and ASTM A952/A952M and ASTM A973/A973M standards
  • Proof tested at minimum 2 times Working Load Limit with certification
  • A-1337 LOK-A-LOY® 10 Connecting Links: Suitable for both Grade 80 and 100 chains
  • Locking system for simple assembly/disassembly - no special tools needed
  • Forged alloy steel - Quenched & Tempered
