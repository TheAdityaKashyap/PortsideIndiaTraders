Turnbuckle Overview

Crosby® Turnbuckles are engineered for superior performance in demanding tensioning applications. Available in various configurations including Hook-Hook, Eye-Eye, Jaw-Eye, and Jaw-Jaw. These hot-dip galvanized steel turnbuckles are designed for maximum strength and durability.

TURNBUCKLES RECOMMENDED FOR STRAIGHT OR IN-LINE PULL ONLY.

Heat-Treated

Quenched & Tempered fittings

Durable Finish

Hot-dip galvanized steel

Fatigue Resistant

20,000 cycles at 1.5x WLL

Safety Certified

5:1 Design Factor

Key Features

  • End fittings are Quenched & Tempered or normalized, bodies heat-treated by normalizing
  • Hot-dip galvanized steel construction
  • Modified UNJ thread on end fittings for improved fatigue properties
  • Body has UNC threads
  • Lock nuts available for all sizes
  • Fatigue rated to 20,000 cycles at 1-1/2 times the Working Load Limit
  • Meets or exceeds all requirements of ASME B30.26 including identification, ductility, design factor, proof load and temperature requirements
  • Meets performance requirements of Federal Specifications FF-T-791b and ASTM F-1145
  • 5:1 Design Factor with Proof Load at 2.5 times the Working Load Limit

Technical Specifications

  • Design Factor: 5:1
  • Proof Load: 2.5 times the Working Load Limit
  • Fatigue Rating: 20,000 cycles at 1.5 times WLL
  • Materials: Hot-dip galvanized steel
  • Threads: Modified UNJ on end fittings, UNC on bodies
  • Certifications: ASME B30.26, Federal Specifications FF-T-791b, ASTM F-1145
