Lowering Belt Overview

Our lowering belts are manufactured from duplex polyester webbing slings joined together with heavy-duty steel terminals, designed for high load capacity when lowering pipes. They offer high safety, low elasticity, and resistance to ultraviolet rays, ensuring long-term reliability in demanding conditions.
