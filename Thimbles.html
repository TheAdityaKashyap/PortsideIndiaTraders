Important Notes

  • All thimbles are forged from high-grade steel for maximum strength
  • Inspect thimbles regularly for signs of wear, deformation, or corrosion
  • Match thimble size to wire rope diameter for optimal performance
  • For heavy loads, consider one size larger thimble
  • Replace when groove depth reduces by 10%

Dimension Guide

Dimension Definitions

  • A - Overall Length
  • B - Inside Width
  • C - Rope Groove Width
  • D - Throat Opening
  • E - Pin Hole Diameter
  • F - Groove Depth
  • G - Groove Radius
  • H - Thickness
  • J - Shackle Compatibility (SL series)

Selection Tips

  • Match thimble size to wire rope diameter
  • For heavy loads, consider one size larger
  • Shackle-Loc models provide direct connection
  • Inspect for proper seating during installation
  • Replace when groove depth reduces by 10%
