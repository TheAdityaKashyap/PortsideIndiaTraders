Dimension Guide

Dimension Definitions

  • A - Overall Length
  • B - Inside Width
  • C - Rope Groove Width
  • D - Throat Opening
  • E - Pin Hole Diameter
  • F - Groove Depth
  • G - Groove Radius
  • H - Thickness
  • J - Shackle Compatibility (SL series)

Selection Tips

  • Match thimble size to wire rope diameter
  • For heavy loads, consider one size larger
  • Shackle-Loc models provide direct connection
  • Inspect for proper seating during installation
  • Replace when groove depth reduces by 10%
