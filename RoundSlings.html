Round Slings Overview

Our round slings are manufactured from high-tenacity polyester yarn, offering superior strength, flexibility, and durability for a wide range of industrial lifting applications. Designed with safety as the top priority, these slings feature customizable safety factors and are rigorously tested to exceed industry standards.

Versatile Lifting

Ideal for delicate, high-value, or irregularly shaped loads with excellent load protection

Compact Design

Low weight-to-strength ratio makes them easy to handle and store

Safety First

Available with 6:1 or 7:1 safety factors to meet diverse safety requirements

Custom Solutions

Special treatments available including fluorescent colors and light-fast styles
