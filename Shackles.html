Shackles Overview

Our Crosby® shackles are forged, quenched, and tempered with alloy pins for superior strength. Designed with safety as the top priority, these shackles feature a 6:1 design factor and are rigorously tested to exceed industry standards. Look for the Red Pin® - the mark of genuine Crosby quality.

Anchor Shackles

Ideal for rigging applications where a larger bow provides more room for multiple connections.

Chain Shackles

Designed with a narrower bow width for fitting to chain links and other confined spaces.

Screw Pin Shackles

Quick and easy to assemble, perfect for temporary connections and non-permanent applications.

Bolt Type Shackles

Secure and permanent connections with nut and bolt assemblies for maximum safety.

Grade 6 Performance

All shackles meet or exceed Grade 6 performance requirements with permanent WLL markings.

Temperature Rated

Approved for use at extreme temperatures from -40°F (-40°C) to 400°F (204°C).

Fatigue Rated

Rated at 1.5 times the Working Load Limit at 20,000 cycles for extended durability.

Safety Factor

6:1 Design Factor with maximum proof load at 2 times the Working Load Limit.

Technical Specifications

All Crosby shackles feature a 6:1 Design Factor with maximum proof load at 2 times the Working Load Limit. Hot-dip galvanized (G) or self-colored (S) finishes available.
